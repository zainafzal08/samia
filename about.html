About me

Hello! I’m Samia.

I didn’t always know I wanted to be a Designer. My love for designing well thought-out product experiences came from learning all about customer experience and service design whilst working at the Apple store.‍

Along with my love for design and great experiences, human behaviour also always interested me, I wanted to understand people at a deeper level and why they behave the way they do, and in the hopes of helping people I majored in Psychology. I very quickly learned I was too empathetic to become a Clinical Psychologist and couldn’t desensitise myself from the problems people faced.

I find the full customer experience fascinating, being in awe of beautiful user experiences in different products led me to research and dig deeper in understanding why I found all this so fulfilling.

Finally, I found my calling of Product Design where I am able to bring my love for beautiful experiences, good design and human behaviour together.

he journey so far has been great and I can’t wait for what comes next. When not designing, I am an illustrator, the occasional artist, lover of good coffee, taking photos of the places I travel to and usually make my friends fat with my love for cooking different cuisines. Oh and I love a good cardio workout mainly to burn off the cooking.‍

Let’s chat about design, illustration, food or travel, drop me an email and say hi!
